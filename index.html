No.3

ELEVATE
BY REEBOK

Elevate, an all in one fitness app, was designed to create better experiences for new and fresh people starting their fitness journeys. Since the fitness industry is booming, and Reebok turned a different direction with their rebrand to cele-brate the everyday person, I wanted to create a fitness app that was designed for experienced and inexperienced users.

NEGOTIATION
SYSTEM

One of the projects that I was tasked to work on at DoubleDoor Technologies was to ideate and design the negotiation system between buyers and sellers within the website. The sprint lasted 2 weeks and involved designing the dashboard, experience of making an offer, and the counter offer experience.

ABOUT ME

I am an Interaction Designer with a degree in psychology. My passion for design and usability stems from opportunity. Opportunity to identify products that can be improved upon, opportunity to actively seek ways to improve it, and opportunity to change a mundane routine or extraneous task through design.
